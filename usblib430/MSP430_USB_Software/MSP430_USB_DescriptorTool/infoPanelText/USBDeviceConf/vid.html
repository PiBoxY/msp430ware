Vendor ID

Associated descriptor field: idVendor (in the Device Descriptor)

The VID is a 16-bit value assigned by the USB Implementers Forum (USB-IF, at www.usb.org). Enter the value in the format 0xnnnn, where nnnn is a four-digit hex number.

If your company has released USB products before, it may already have a VID. If not, it can be obtained by:

  • Becoming a member of the USB-IF. This involves a $4000 annual fee. (http://www.usb.org/developers/vendor/)
  • Licensing one from the USB-IF. This costs $2000 for a two-year license. (http://www.usb.org/developers/vendor/)
  • Licensing use of a single PID within TI's VID space (VID 0x2047). (http://www.ti.com/430usb) This is available at no charge.

If you haven't done this, and just want to experiment with the device or are just starting your development, TI-MSP430 has allocated a range of PIDs you can use within our VID space. Use:

  • VID = 0x2047
  • PID = click the PID field to see its Help Pane text.
