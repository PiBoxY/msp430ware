T10 Strings

Reported in the LUN's response to INQUIRY SCSI commands

T10 is the standards body that defines SCSI. Vendors are supposed to have a unique T10 vendor ID (VID), but there isn't much enforcement of this. At this time, there is no fee to obtain a T10 Vendor ID. http://www.t10.org/

The product ID (PID) and revision are up to the vendor's discretion.

Since these fields have little effect, and yet values are required, a checkbox is provided that automatically derives values from the USB VID/PID/revision.
